Agno and Shawarma

March 11, 2020 • Hans Lao

Food

The cheapest food in Agno are the instant noodles for ₱20. Although, if you want to take out, you need to add an additional ₱5. I haven’t a personal favorite, but the stall I keep going back to is the stall on the far left and far back. Unfortunately, I know not their name, but their food is good.

Now, as of now (February 2020), Gokongwei Hall hasn’t a place for students to sit down and wait for their next classes. Rather, computer science and engineering students wait at Andrew. The closest food stall to Andrew is the shawarma place in front of 7-Eleven. Like the instant noodles place, this place charges an additional ₱5 for take out. While their food is good, the portions are lacking.
