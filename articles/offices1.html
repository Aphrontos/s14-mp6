Registrar, Accounting, and Faculties

March 11, 2020 • Hans Lao

Offices

For a student, there are only 3 offices that you need: Registrar, Accounting, and Faculty.

Accounting has 2 offices, one in St. La Salle Hall (ground floor) and the other in Henry Sy Hall (2nd floor). Henry Sy Hall (2nd floor) also has the Registrar’s office.

There are 4 areas where the faculties are concentrated. Faculties for the sciences (biology, physics, and so on) are in William Hall. Falculties for humanities (linguistics, religion, and so on) are in the Faculty Center. The faculty for computer science is in Andrew (10th floor). The faculty for physical education is in Razon (7th floor).
